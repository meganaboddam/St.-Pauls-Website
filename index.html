Our Food Pantry

In addressing the food services of our community last year we opened a Food Pantry. We initially relied solely on the generosity of our parishioners and other food donations from friends and family of St. Paul’s. We offer food services for anyone requesting our services regardless of their ethnicity or religious affiliation. Our Parish is located in the South Bronx, one of the poorest areas of the City.

We serve an average of 100 clients per month with 2 bags for each family.

Our grocery bags contain a balanced meal of non-perishable food items.

Our Mission

St. Paul’s Episcopal church is a member of the Anglican Communion. We are a great worshipping, nurturing, mission oriented and Christ centered community. We strive to live according to our Baptismal Covenant; to walk, and live according to the life and teaching of Jesus Christ and to be a welcoming community for all of God’s children.
